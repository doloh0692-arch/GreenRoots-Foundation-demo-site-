Who We Are

Rooted in purpose,
growing since 2009

GreenRoots Foundation was born from a simple belief: healthy ecosystems make healthy communities. We work at the intersection of environmental conservation, wildlife rescue, and environmental education — because real change needs roots that go deep.

Science-driven conservation Every program is grounded in ecological research and measurable outcomes.
Community-first approach We partner with local communities, because they are nature's best stewards.
Radical transparency Donors see exactly how every dollar is used — no exceptions.
What We Do

Three pillars,
one mission

🌳

Forest Conservation

We protect and restore threatened forests through land acquisition, reforestation campaigns, and anti-deforestation advocacy. Our teams have planted over 2 million native trees across 14 regions.

Fund This Program →
🦋

Wildlife Rescue & Rehab

Our licensed wildlife centers rescue injured and displaced animals, provide veterinary care, and release them back into protected habitats. We've helped over 8,400 individual animals since 2012.

📚

Environmental Education

From school curricula to adult workshops, we equip people with the knowledge to live more sustainably. Our mobile education units reach remote communities with no access to formal schooling.
